Specs:
- Aspect ratio: 4:5 portrait
- Minimum: 1200 × 1500 (2400 × 3000 preferred for retina)
- Format: optimized JPG, target < 300 KB per image
- Subject framing: same angle, same crop, same lighting, same background between before and after. Mismatched framing breaks the slider illusion immediately.
- Color tone: warm and natural. Avoid over-edited "TV commercial white teeth" finish.

CRITICAL — Legal & regulatory:
- All images must be from real Biodonth patients with signed CFO-compliant consent forms on file.
- No stock photography pretending to be patients.
- No exaggerated retouching (CFO advertising rules require honest representation of results).
