# Video poster frames

Place the institutional video poster at:

`biodonth-tour-poster.jpg`

Specs:
- Aspect ratio: 16:9
- Minimum: 1600 × 900 (2400 × 1350 preferred for retina)
- Format: optimized JPG, target < 200 KB
- Subject: a strong still from the video — interior of the clinic, reception, or a candid moment. Should look cinematic, not clinical.

The poster is shown before the user clicks play. It is the user's first impression of the video — choose carefully.
